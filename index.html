FM WhatsApp

FM WhatsApp is a modified version of WhatsApp that offers additional features and customization options beyond what the official app provides.

Features of FM WhatsApp:

  • Customizable themes with options for font, background, and colors.
  • Increased privacy options such as hiding online status, blue ticks, and typing status.
  • More emoji and sticker options, including access to thousands of custom stickers.
  • Enhanced file sharing capabilities, allowing you to send larger files up to 1GB.
  • Anti-delete messages feature that prevents others from deleting messages sent to you.
  • Message scheduling to send messages at a specific time automatically.
  • Multiple accounts support, enabling you to use two WhatsApp accounts on the same device.
  • Built-in app lock to secure your chats with a password or fingerprint.
  • And much more!

How to Install FM WhatsApp?

  1. Download the latest version of FM WhatsApp APK from the official website or a trusted source.
  2. Before installing, ensure that you have allowed installation from unknown sources in your device settings.
  3. Locate the downloaded APK file and tap on it to begin the installation process.
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation.
  5. Once installed, open FM WhatsApp and verify your phone number.
  6. Restore your chats from the backup if prompted.
  7. Customize your FM WhatsApp settings according to your preferences.
  8. You're now ready to enjoy all the extra features FM WhatsApp has to offer!
